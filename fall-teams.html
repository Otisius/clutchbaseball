Fall 2025 Baseball – 12U & 13U Teams

We're excited to announce that our Fall season will include 12U and 13U teams! Tryouts will take place in June — please use the interest form below to be notified of the exact date and location.

12U

Players born on/after May 1, 2013

13U

Players born on/after May 1, 2012

Each team will include:

  • 1 field practice per week
  • 1 indoor hitting & conditioning session per week
  • 3–4 tournaments
  • Additional scrimmage games throughout the season
Feel free to share this opportunity with others in your Little League community — we're always looking for dedicated players ready to grow their game!
