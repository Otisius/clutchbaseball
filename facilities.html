Our Facilities

We're proud to train at a variety of high-quality locations that support both skill development and team training. Our primary facilities include turf fields throughout the San Mateo school district, Barrel Up in Belmont — a premier indoor training center — and Woodside Priory High School. With flexible training locations across the Peninsula, we're able to reach players in multiple areas and offer convenient access to top-tier coaching and development opportunities.

San Mateo School District Turf Fields

Full-size turf fields providing excellent playing and practice surfaces for team training and development.

Barrel Up – Belmont

A premier indoor training center equipped with batting cages and professional-grade training equipment for hitting and conditioning sessions.

Woodside Priory High School

Home field of Head Coach Kyle Barret's varsity program, offering top-quality baseball infrastructure on the Peninsula.
